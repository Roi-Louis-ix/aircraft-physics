Among the most common engine types are reciprocating, turboprop, turbofan, turbojet, and scramjet. These engines have their own applications depending on efficiency and performance.

Reciprocating (piston) engines

Reciprocating engines are found in smaller aircraft, especially training ones. As the heading suggests, it is made from a piston engine similar to the one which can be found in an automobile.
