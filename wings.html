Drag Force and its Components

Another force created by the wing is drag, which resists motion. It is caused by the movement of any object through a fluid. There are three types of drag, which are skin friction drag, form drag, and wave drag.

Skin friction drag is the friction between the object's surface and the fluid around it, whereof the fluid is air in our situation. Form drag is the drag arising from the object's shape. Wave drag is caused by shock waves, which are formed as part of an object travels at supersonic speeds. This affects the drag felt by faster aircraft. Drag is modeled by this equation:
